About Me

My name is Toni Lomax I am 25 years old. I was born in West Philadelphia and moved to West Mt. Airy when I was four. I am a writer, artist and soon to be web developer. For high school I went to The Philadelphia High School for Creative and Performing Arts. After high school I went to The Catholic University of America. There I studied Art and Italian History and Language. From there I went to school in Canada at the Ontario College of Art and Design where I studied Fine Arts. I am very happy to be studying web development at UPenn LPS Bootcamp and look forward to filling up my portfolio with awesome projects!
